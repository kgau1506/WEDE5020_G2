Skin Type Guide Preview

Understanding your skin type is the first step into building an effective skincare routine. Our Skin Type Guide page helps you identify whether you have normal, dry, oily, combination or sensitive skin and it also explains the characteristics of each type of skin.

It also provides recommendations on products suitable and ingredients that are suitable for each skin type, including guidance for individuals with specific skin needs such as sensitivity, albinism and vitiligo. This ensures that every individual can find a routine that works great for their skin.

Skincare routine

Our Skincare Routine page provides a step by step guide on how to properly care for your skin both in the morning and at night. It explains the correct order of applying products such as cleansers, toners, serums, moisturisers and sunscreen.

The page also includes helpful tips on maintaining consistency, avoiding common skincare mistakes and achieving the best results over time. This guide is developed to make skincare simple, easy to follow and effective for all users.
